🎯 Our Mission

We believe AI tools are only as powerful as the prompts that guide them. PromptVault was created to solve a real problem: finding high-quality, tested prompts for the right AI tool takes hours of trial and error.

Our mission is to make AI accessible to everyone — from professional designers to curious beginners — by providing a curated, organised, and constantly growing library of prompts that actually work.

🚀 What We Offer

  • Curated Prompt Library — Every prompt is hand-selected and tested for real results
  • Category & Subcategory Filtering — Find exactly what you need: Image › Couple, Video › Cinematic, Writing › Blog, and more
  • Multi-tool Support — Prompts for Midjourney, DALL·E 3, Stable Diffusion, ChatGPT, Claude, Sora, Runway ML and more
  • Free & Premium — Hundreds of free prompts available instantly. Premium prompts unlockable via a short ad in the app
  • Android App — Browse, save and copy prompts from your phone
  • Always Updated — New prompts added regularly as AI tools evolve
